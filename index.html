MIDI Viewer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 April 2026

Overview

MIDI Viewer is a performance utility for viewing drum pad assignments on Roland SPD-SX PRO and compatible devices. The app is designed with privacy in mind — it does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

MIDI Viewer does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No network requests (the app works entirely offline)
  • No access to contacts, photos, location, or other device data

Local Data

The app stores kit configuration files (YAML format) on your device only. These files contain drum pad labels and settings imported from your MIDI device backup. This data never leaves your device.

MIDI Device Communication

The app communicates with connected MIDI devices (e.g. Roland SPD-SX PRO) via USB using standard MIDI and SysEx protocols. This communication is local to your device and does not involve any network transmission.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases and management are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). MIDI Viewer does not process or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
